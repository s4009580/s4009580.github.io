Interactive Experience Research Project

1) What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience? The experience opens with asking you to change the size of your browser to see the “sun rise”. Along with that, there is a beautiful, harmonised colour, landscape and a helicopter overlooking the pink sunset on the dock with palm trees framing as a silhouette. The visuals were the first thing I noticed upon opening the site.

2) Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions. Reading the text “resize your browser”, I follow the instructions to achieve progression Making the browser as small in width as possible, I proceed clicking “start”. Skimming through the tutorial, I mainly picked up: - click and drag scrollbar at bottom to progress - click and drag mouse to move around the frame - click and hold to immerse in audio experience (later on, I realise you should be looking out for the special interactive feature + utilising the poetic interlude) I click and drag around the experience, using the scroller to progress
